About Me

I received a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from RMIT University, where Professor Zahir Tari supervised me. I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Computer engineering (software) and an Honours degree in computer science. I am currently a lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Cybersecurity discipline, School of Information Technologies, Deakin University (Australia). I am interested in research topics in the area of systems security and scalability/performance. In particular, my research interests include:

  • Systems’ security (such as decentralized systems, Cloud/edge-based systems) and Network security.
  • Scalability and performance in large-scale distributed systems (such as blockchains, and more specifically, consensus).

You can also find me on GoogleScholar, LinkedIn, DBLP, ResearchGate.

I’m always looking for grad/undergrad students with a strong background in the field of system security and performance/scalability. If you are interested in system research, email me.
